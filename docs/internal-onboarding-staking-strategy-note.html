Internal Strategy Note

Emorya Gamify: Onboarding, Staking, and Monetisation Readiness

This note sets out a tighter product direction for the most important user journey in Emorya Gamify: taking a curious spectator, turning them into a fully activated Emorya user, and then moving them toward monetisation readiness.

It is intended as an internal strategy note rather than a pitch document. The goal is to align the team on why onboarding and staking should become a more deliberate, heavily weighted progression system.

Core Position

The North Star for the platform is user acquisition. More specifically, it is the conversion of curious spectators into everyday Emorya users.

With that in mind, the onboarding path is not a minor starter layer. It is the single most important conversion bridge in the product. If we believe the platform’s purpose is to move people from interest into real use, then the full path from first app download through to wallet connection and first real in-app action should be treated as a high-value gamified ladder.

The key design principle is this: onboarding should be rewarded heavily, but the reward curve should be back-loaded. The biggest onboarding reward should go to full activation completion, not simply to the first step.

Why This Matters

  • It gives the platform a much clearer acquisition function rather than just a campaign function.
  • It creates a visible psychological path from curiosity to identity, activation, commitment, and growth.
  • It improves the chances that a new user actually finishes onboarding instead of stopping midway.
  • It makes wallet connection feel like a real milestone rather than a technical side-step.
  • It gives Gamify a stronger role in moving users toward monetisation readiness.

Master Progression Model

1. Spectator

Curious, watching content, not yet committed.

  • join communities
  • follow socials
  • download app

2. Connected User

Identity begins forming inside the ecosystem.

  • open app
  • create account
  • complete profile
  • connect xPortal

3. Activated User

Starts using the product for real.

  • view reward path
  • first in-app action
  • first calorie conversion

4. Retained User

Returns repeatedly and builds habits.

  • daily wheel
  • adventure game
  • streaks and challenge loops

5. Monetisation-Ready User

Moves from engagement into economic commitment.

  • upgrade monthly
  • upgrade annual
  • stake EMR
  • maintain stake

6. Growth User

Helps drive trust, visibility, and acquisition.

  • referrals
  • reviews
  • UGC
  • milestone shares

Onboarding As A Dedicated Activation Ladder

The strongest version of onboarding is not a loose collection of starter quests. It is a named activation ladder with escalating value at each stage.

Step Suggested XP Why It Matters
Download the Emorya app 75 XP First meaningful commitment step.
Open the app for the first time 100 XP Confirms movement from spectator to active interest.
Create Emorya account 200 XP Core identity conversion moment.
Complete your profile 150 XP Improves user quality and lowers future drop-off.
Confirm your starter setup 125 XP Closes the setup loop cleanly.
Download xPortal 125 XP Prepares the user for reward and wallet readiness.
Open or create your xPortal wallet 175 XP Increases seriousness of onboarding.
Connect Emorya to your xPortal wallet 300 XP One of the most important activation milestones.
View your EMRS reward path 125 XP Teaches how actions connect to value.
Complete your first real in-app action 250 XP Moves the user beyond setup into use.
Convert your first calories 350 XP True activation proof point.
Complete the full activation ladder 750 XP The real spectator-to-user conversion moment.

This structure intentionally puts the largest reward at the end of onboarding. That gives the user meaningful incentive to finish the journey rather than stopping after one or two low-effort steps.

Why Staking Should Be The Next Major Ladder

Once a user is activated and retained, the next strategic move is to guide them into deeper commitment. Staking is the cleanest bridge from engagement into monetisation readiness.

If a user must have EMR staked in order to monetise, then staking is not just another wallet task. It becomes a major psychological and economic transition point:

  • engage
  • earn
  • stake
  • unlock stronger benefits
  • become monetisation-ready

APY Boost Incentive

XP and progression rewards make staking visible inside Gamify, but an APY boost above a defined staking threshold would make it materially more meaningful. That would give users a real economic reason to move from casual participation into stronger commitment.

This would add value in several ways:

  • stronger staking participation
  • clearer EMR utility
  • stronger retention among serious users
  • positive impact on the “Road to Healthy” staking campaign
  • a much clearer path from engagement to monetisation readiness

The cleanest structure is threshold-based rather than linear. That is easier to explain, easier to balance, and stronger psychologically.

Staking Step Suggested XP Purpose
Stake your first EMR 250 XP First commitment step into the staking journey.
Reach staking threshold A 400 XP Move from symbolic to meaningful stake size.
Reach staking threshold B 900 XP Unlock APY boost eligibility and deeper commitment status.
Unlock APY boost status 600 XP Make the economic incentive explicit inside Gamify.
Maintain qualifying stake for 30 days 1200 XP Reward commitment over time, not just a staking snapshot.
Referred staker bonus 500 XP Add growth leverage to the staking ladder.

Recommended Product Framing

Onboarding should be framed as the activation ladder. Staking should be framed as the monetisation-readiness ladder. Together, they create a much stronger overall product story:

  • Gamify drives user behavior.
  • Onboarding converts curiosity into activation.
  • Staking converts activation into economic commitment.
  • APY and longer-term benefits convert commitment into retention.

Recommended Next Product Steps

  • formalise the onboarding ladder as a named activation sequence inside the quest framework
  • expand the number of real in-Emorya app events that can complete onboarding and activation tasks
  • define the exact xPortal and wallet-link journey we want to reward
  • agree internally on staking thresholds and whether APY boost above threshold is commercially feasible
  • add staking quests as a dedicated commitment ladder rather than a single isolated task
  • connect milestone-share mechanics later so onboarding and staking wins can be shared publicly

Bottom Line

The strongest next evolution of Emorya Gamify is not a broad new product layer. It is making the most valuable existing journey much more deliberate.

If onboarding becomes a high-value activation ladder, and staking becomes a high-value monetisation-readiness ladder, the whole platform becomes more strategically coherent. It stops being a collection of quests and becomes a clear progression system from spectator to committed ecosystem participant.
